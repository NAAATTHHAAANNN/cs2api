Welcome to cs2api!

To access all skins: /cs2api/skins

To access list of all collections: /cs2api/collections

To access specific collection: /cs2api/collections/kilowatt

To access skin by name: /cs2api/skins/USP-S%20Jawbreaker %20 is a space placeholder.

To access skin by name in specific collection: /cs2api/collections/kilowatt/USP-S%20Jawbreaker %20 is a space placeholder.
