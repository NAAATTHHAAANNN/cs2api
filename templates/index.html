Welcome to cs2api!

To access all skins: /cs2api/skins

To access skin by id: /cs2api/skins/search?id=1

To access list of all collections: /cs2api/collections

To access specific collection: /cs2api/collections/kilowatt

To access skin by name: /cs2api/skins/search/n?name=M4A1-S | Vaporwave %20 is a space placeholder.
